Sigma Phi Delta Beta Kappa

March 3, 2022 Executive Board

It's been almost 10 years since Lehigh's Sigma Phi Delta Beta Kappa Chapter was founded. Since our founding, our chapter has grown immensely, and we are very proud to boast the accomplishments of the chapter and our brothers.

Our organization continues to foster an environment where young men can make the most out of the Lehigh expirience. Professionally, we help each other face the rigors of engineering, while refining our resumes, seeking out summer opportunities, and fine tuning our presentation skills and professional appearance. Socially, we have continued to integrate into the Greek community and have made many lifelong friends along the way.

The chapter has been in the process of trying to get a house on the hill since 2019. We are incredibly excited to announce we will be moving into house 96 this summer! We will have 29 of our brothers living in the facility in the fall and will be at a 97% capacity. This accomplishment means the world to the brotherhood. We will finally have a place that is actually large enough for us to all gather for social and brotherhood events, meals, and study hours.

With the new chapter house, comes a new set of challenges. The first of which, is furnishing the house. If you would like to donate to our chapter so that we can make this transition as smooth as possible, please click on the donate button below!
